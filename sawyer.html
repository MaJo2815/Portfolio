Sawyer The Squirrel

Battle for nuts

You are a red squirrel somewhere in Great Britain, and your goal is to defend your nuts from grey squirrels who are looking to take your nuts, the easy way or the hard way.

Sawyer The Squirrel is a student videogame created by Angel Risso, Gerardo Portillo, Gina Funes, Ricardo Moncada, Roberto Ordóñez and María Fuentes between January 2023 and March 2023 in a 10 week span.

My role: Concept artist, 3D character modeler, 3D props modeler and 3D character animator.

Academic adviser: Luis Fernando Lee.

You can download the game https://syvergamedev.itch.io/sawyer-the-squirrel
