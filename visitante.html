Pequeño Visitante

(A small visitor)

Dusky is taking an exam to obtain his "frightener's license", but the presence of an old man will change the course of his evaluation. Will Dusky approve his exam?

Pequeño Visitante is a student animated shortfilm created by María Fuentes and produced between January 2023 and March 2023 in a 13 week span.

Directed, produced and animated by: María Fuentes

Soundtrack: Cecilia Bu

Academic advisers: Ana Isabel Martins, Taiki Kawano and Mario Escobar.
