About me

Nice to meet you! I'm María Fuentes, an animation student at UNITEC (Universidad Tecnológica Centroamericana) in Tegucigalpa, Honduras.

I'm the creator of the short film Pequeño Visitante, 3D modeler and animator in the game Sawyer The Squirrel and currently working on the production of a shortfilm called Sonrisa Perdida, as a screenwriter, 3D modeler and animator.

My main purpose as a future animator is to tell compelling stories that could inspire people around the world aknowleding animation as a medium to transmit powerful ideas. Therefore, I'm always looking to improve myself everytime, searching for new opportunities and challenges that can help me to improve my abilities in 3D modeling and 3D Animation.

You can contact my by email: fuentesmaria@unitec.edu
